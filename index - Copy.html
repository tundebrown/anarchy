JOIN A CLAN

HYBRIDS

This is the cluster of some rare mutants that have come together to form this clan. They have come into existence by combining two races/entities like alien+human, animal+human, alien+animal, human+machine, animal+machine, and a lot more. They were created with the idea of overcoming the weaknesses of either or both races. These distinctive combinations make them highly competitive in the battle for dominance.

AI BOTS

This bunch of scientifically developed robots is arguably one of the most fascinating warrior clans. Coming into existence after hundreds of years of research and development, they are capable of becoming a nightmare for their enemies. With most of the world taken over by AI, they have one of the most advanced forces to exist. They have a keen eye on bringing a new dawn to a completely AI-dominated world. All calculations & no emotions make them super lethal.

HUMANS

Coming into existence millions of years after the big bang, evolving from apes to being one of the smartest creatures now, humans are rightfully the strong contenders for the ultimate position of power. They have been continuously evolving for thousands of years since their existence. Their natural habitat Earth being chosen as the battlefield for the first season, humans have a slight edge over the other clans.

TIME TRAVELERS

They are one of the most sought-after clans to exist. Some of them have come into the present from thousands of years in the past whereas some of them are from thousands of years from the future. Time travelling no longer be a distant dream, this set of warriors with their bag of mysteries are looking out to wipe the other races out of existence and be at the apex, where they think they originally belong.

INTERGALACTIC WARRIORS

This blended bunch of extraterrestrial creatures has come into being as new species were discovered on a plethora of different planets and galaxies over the span of a million years and still counting. These still evolving enigmatic creatures are the ones to look out for as they are hungry for power and desperate to prove their superiority amongst all other clans.

SUPERNATURALS

All the Myths that you have heard of - Vampires, Druids, Angels, Demons etc. This clan consists of a set of interdimensional supernatural creatures with a load of bizarre history behind them which is responsible for their rise into existence. Being one of the most exciting clans with ‘mystery’ as their boon, they are more than ready for any battle that comes in their way to establish complete dominance.
